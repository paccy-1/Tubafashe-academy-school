Our curriculum blends play-based learning with structured academics, fostering curiosity and foundational skills. Early years emphasize literacy and numeracy through interactive games, storytelling, and sensory activities, while primary students engage in project-based STEM/STEAM programs and collaborative problem-solving.

Our Approach

Inspired by the Reggio Emilia approach, we view children as capable explorers and teachers as guides. Our student-centered classrooms encourage inquiry, hands-on discovery, and peer collaboration, nurturing independence and a lifelong love of learning.

Program Structure

Focus on social skills, motor development, and language through play, music, and outdoor exploration. Daily activities include sensory play, story time, and creative movement.

Structured yet flexible learning with core subjects enriched by coding, drama, and environmental studies. Features project-based learning and leadership opportunities.
